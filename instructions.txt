Title: Student Database System
Tools used: Python, built-in libraries

Features:

1. add student (add a student to the database)
2. add grade (adds a grade to a certain student)
3. display (displays all students nicely)
4. display averages (displays student grade averages)
5. adjust grades (adjusts grades for all students)
6. top/bottom students (displats top and bottom student averages)
7. delete (deletes a student)
8. count students (counts students in database)
9. leave (exits the program)
